多行的大括号

$$ W[i,j]= max \left\{ \\
\begin{aligned}
	P[i,j], \\
	max_{1\leq x\leq i,1\leq y \leq j} W[x,y] + max(
	W[x,j-y]+W[i-x,j], 
	& W[i-x,y]+W[i.j-y])
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
